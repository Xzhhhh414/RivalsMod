1. Open up Editor.exe, found in emedit-editor-beta-windows.

2. When it's opened, click the three bars at the top near "Recent projects".

3. Click preferences, scroll to the bottom, and then click on GML dialects directory.

4. You should now be in a folder called "api" found in your Roaming AppData folder. Drag and drag the roa folder into there.

5. Reload GMEdit
